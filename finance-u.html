finance u

Case Study

Project Type

UX Design + UI Design

Tools Used

User interviews, Market research, Competitive Analysis, Affinity Map & HMW, Problem Statement, Site Audit, Worst Idea, Crazy 8 ideation, Information Architecture, Sitemap, Lo-fi Wireframes, Mi-fi prototyping & Usability Testing, Brand attributes & moodboard, Style Tile, Hi-fi prototyping, Figma

Duration

15 days

The Challenge

Created in 2018, finance4u is a software provider for the wealth management industry. Our solutions allow our clients to digitize the investment process, from client on boarding to due diligence and online subscription.

The current version of our Saas Software has a basic mostly functional/Dev oriented design. And we would like to make into more user-friendly and easy to use design.

The Project

This is our problematic :Active Private equity needs to find a way to use the digital platform for documentation and regulation because they have physical data in an unorganized way.

User Research :The first thing we did was to create a poll using Google Forms and send it out to some of our friends and groups.

During the study phase, we also interviewed five users. They told us more about their behavior, experiences in equity, and pain points. Each interview was conducted through the Zoom app. Here are a few quotes from the interview:

“The platform is not simple as we use now like amazon and other sites”

John

“No single platform that shows the online market and dashboard analytics. we have a switch between two platforms to know the graph and analytics of data we invested”

Shelton

Affinity Diagram :We used an affinity diagram to discover the patterns and similarities among the different interviews. The results allowed us to recognize that our private equity and wealth managers and the main pain points are:

  1. No single platform that shows the online market and dashboard analytics.
  2. More importantly, I would like to have a visualization of Graphic for my equity on the dashboard.
  3. Alert is not reaching me when I was not inside the platform.

Minimum Viable Product :The focus was to build an MVP, so narrowing down the must-haves was crucial at this point in the process.

The goal of the Finance4u Platform, at the bare minimum, is to make users report their funds and monitor their digital regulation through this platform and also provide visualization of arithmetic calculation on their reports.

Provide Demo to investors includes all of the view portal like client, asset, product, and distributor.

we provided digital documentation for all investors and Private equity.By ensuring that these things are available, the user can use this platform user-friendly and also able to review and notification that is available to them by this platform. whether they are on the platform or outside.

decided to focus on a SAAS DESKTOP version of the Application with just enough features to be usable by early customers.

My main focus was to work on the information architecture and UX writing.
